SMITE has 6 different casual gamemodes and 3 competetive ones.
All the 6 gamemodes are:
  • Conquest (5v5 3 lanes)

  • Arena (1 arena 5v5)

  • Joust (3v3 1 lane)

  • Clash (5v5 2 lanes)

  • Siege (4v4 2 lanes)

  • GOTW (rotating random gamemode)


Conquest: Conquest is the most 'normal' gamemode across MOBA's, a 5v5 in 3 lanes and a jungle.
Arena: Arena is the most casual gamemode in SMITE, simply because its not in the competetive scene and allround fun to play with friends.
Joust: Joust is a 3v3 gamemode with 1 lane, its mostly played by parties of 3 friends trying to have fun. This gamemode is also in the competetive scene.
Clash: Clash is a more competetively played gamemode across SMITE, its a fast paced, high pressure gamemode for people to play in. Not for everyone
Siege: Siege is a not so much played gamemode, but still a fun one to play with friends. It's 4v4 in 2 lanes and for really casual players.
GOTW: GOTW stands for Game Of The Week and is a rotating fun gamemode to try new, random modes given to or created by the SMITE community.
