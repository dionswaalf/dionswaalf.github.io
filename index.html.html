SMITE is a first person MOBA where you fight with your teammates against 5 enemies.
The objective of the game is to break all enemy defences and killing their titan by attacking it.
The defences used in the main gamemode are 6 towers, 2 in their own lanes, 3 phoenixes and 1 titan.
There are 3 lanes in the main gamemode names Conquest, left lane, right lane and middle lane with a jungle in the middle of all lanes.
There are 5 different classes in smite, each resembling a role and a lane, this all explained in the "Type of gods" menu.
At last, there are 6 different gamemodes in SMITE to vary the gamestyles and gaming experience.
